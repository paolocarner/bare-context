## Graphic Style

**Signature motif:** Fingerprint (eggplant/purple tones) — large decorative background element across all surfaces.  
**Secondary motifs:** Infinity loop (line art), flowing wave lines, organic blob shapes.  
**Accent shapes:** Lush Green half-circle/leaf, Coral circle, Sunshine Yellow circle — geometric accents only.

**Illustration DO:**
- Flat, faceless character illustrations in brand palette
- Geometric organic shapes
- Clean line art in Deep Violet or White

**Illustration DON'T:**
- Photorealistic 3D renders
- Cartoon/chibi characters
- AI-generated complex globes or chaotic diagrams
- Dark/black illustration backgrounds
- Generic cybersecurity clichés (padlocks, shield-on-blue, globe networks)

## Brand Voice

**Tone positioning (bold = BARE's position):**
- Funny ← **Neutral** → Serious
- Casual ← **Friendly** → Formal
- Irreverent ← **Respectful** → Courteous
- Enthusiastic ← **Relaxed** → Matter-of-fact

**Voice pillars:**
- **Knowledgeable yet Approachable** — Expert content with warmth. Clear and professional, never cold or heavy with jargon.
- **Confident and Reassuring** — Clients feel secure. Emphasise reliability and expertise, never fear.
- **Educational** — Help clients understand the *value* of security and how it enables growth. Compliance as a business driver, not a burden.

**Writing principles:** Authentic · Clear · Positive · Friendly · Professional

**UBP Messaging Framework** — every piece of content should do one or more of:
1. **Resonate** — connect with the client's actual strategic challenge
2. **Substantiate** — provide factual, evidence-based proof (FAIR, ENISA data, real metrics)
3. **Differentiate** — make clear why BARE is not a one-size-fits-all vendor
